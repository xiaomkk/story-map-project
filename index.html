Where heat risk is highest

The City’s Heat Vulnerability Index (HVI) highlights neighborhoods facing elevated risk. Darker tracts indicate higher vulnerability based on environmental and social factors.

Swimming pools

Public pools provide seasonal relief on extreme heat days.

Recreation & program sites

PPR program sites (recreation centers, older adult centers, environmental education sites) may serve as cooling resources during emergencies.
